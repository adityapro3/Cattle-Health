Social Awareness on Cattle Healthcare

Frequently asked questions

  • What is Cattle Health?
    Cattle Health is an application which can predict the disease which causes reduction in milk production and are responsible for large number of deaths in cattle..
  • Why Cattle Health?
    Cattle Health helps in early recognition of cattle diseases hence reducing the effect and consequences occurring from the widespread spread of the disease.
  • What is the importance of livestock agricultural?
    Livestock production contributes 4.11 % to India’s national Gross Domestic Product (GDP) and 25.6 % to agricultural GDP.
  • What are the losses occurring from cattle diseases?
    FOR FARMERS
    • Deaths in animals
    • Low yields (e.g., reduction in milk production)

    FOR SOCIETY
    • Deaths in humans
    • Decreased access to food
    • Poor quality animal products
  • What are the effects of cattle diseases in the economy?
    Research has found that cattle disease can increase GHG emissions by up to 24% per unit of milk and 113% per beef carcass. Just one disease, Foot and Mouth, costs farmers up to $21 billion dollars every year. Over just 12 years at the start of the millennium, six international incidents of animal disease caused economic losses of US$ 80 billion.
